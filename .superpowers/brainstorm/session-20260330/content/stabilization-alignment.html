Phase 3: Dependency Health

Stabilization Gate

  • Navigation: 2.10.0-alpha01 → 2.10.x (Stable path check)
  • Lifecycle: 2.11.0-alpha02 → Stable candidate

Goal: Shrink the "Alpha Surface Area" by 30-40% before starting the Android 16/17 modernization.

Verification & Testing Strategy

To ensure this massive migration doesn't break the core streaming experience:

  • Playback Regression Matrix: Every MVI refactor of the Player must pass a "Playback Matrix" (Direct Play, Transcoding, Subtitles, Audio Track switching).
  • TV Focus Checks: Any change to navigation or material libraries requires a D-pad navigation audit on the TV home screen.
  • Screenshot Tests: Capture baseline screenshots of Home/Player before refactoring to detect layout regressions during the MVI transition.
